What is Menpha

Menpha is an open project developed to gather real information just about any uniquely-identifiable device in the world. With this information in hand, Menpha goes a step further to provide you with its core service of helping you find an item of yours should it get lost or stolen. Menpha does so by allowing any one within its vibrant global community to learn about your lost/stolen item, preventing the resale of it.

Registration is free and instant. Only the most important personal information about you is required by Menpha. The rest? Keep them in your private bedroom! Learn about our privacy and terms

How does it work?

We connect users with causes most likely to interest them. Each day, the app highlights cool projects from organizations like yours.

Users donate to projects they like.

A few dollars become many as users invite their friends to donate through social media. The more people who donate, the faster your project gets funded.

Your nonprofit receives monthly project donations through our partner Network for Good.
