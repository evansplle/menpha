What is Menpha?

"In a nutshell, Menpha is a platform to inform you the potential state of the device you wish to purchase - either its a stolen device, or of genuine orgin(s)." - Developer

Menpha, ( pronounced M3n-fa) is a local Akan word in Ghana, which means when transliterated, "Don't take". Menpha is a web app developed to solve one problem of international scale by a Django Developer in Ghana. The service provided by the Menpha web app is simple and one.

In today's world, trading (selling and buying) of smartphones or generally electronic gadgets is the norm. However, Menpha with its platform, meets head-on with this global cancar, by informing its uses of potentially stolen item, giving them a better insight into making the right decision either to purchase a second-hand device or otherwise.
