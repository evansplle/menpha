With Menpha, you are always in the knowing of whether the smartphone or tablet you're planning buying is a missing or stolen one.

Menpha is designed to provide global community-based tracking of IMEI's numbers of devices. Regardless of where you lost your device where you want to buy the awesome device your friend presented, you can help prevent the purchase or buying of it.

Our system can be trusted enough. Until the orginal owner of the device marks it as stolen, its not in your search results, which implies, the original owner still has his/her precious gadget. On the other hand, immediately a user marks his/her gadget as stolen from their personal account, the IMEI of the gadget is publicly available to be searched for, providing you right information with regards to your decision making.
